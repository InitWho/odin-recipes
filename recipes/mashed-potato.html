Mashed Potato

This is a recipe for some damn fine mashed potatoes

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • butter
  • milk
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into small pieces
  2. Boil potatoes in a large pot of water, until soft
  3. Drain potatoes
  4. Add remaining ingredients to the potatoes and mash furiously until creamy
  5. Enjoy
